Privacy Policy

This policy explains how the Google Search Console MCP Server accesses, uses, stores, and protects data when you connect your Google account.

Effective date: June 2, 2026 Applies to: google-search-console-mcp-server.irensaltali.com

1. What this service does

Google Search Console MCP Server is a hosted integration that lets compatible AI clients connect to Google Search Console through OAuth and then call Search Console tools such as property listing, Search Analytics queries, URL Inspection, and sitemap operations.

2. Data we access

When you authorize Google, we may access the Google Search Console data needed to operate the tools you invoke, including:

  • Search Console property identifiers and permission context
  • Search performance data returned by Search Analytics queries
  • URL Inspection results and indexing diagnostics
  • Sitemap metadata and submission status
  • Basic account-linked OAuth tokens required to maintain the connection

We do not request access to Gmail, Drive contents, or unrelated Google account data.

3. How we use the data

We use Google data only to provide the MCP server functionality you requested, including:

  • Authenticating your Google Search Console session
  • Serving requested tool calls to your MCP client
  • Returning Search Console responses back to the client you are using
  • Operating basic security, abuse prevention, and service diagnostics

We do not sell your Google Search Console data and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Data sharing

Your data is shared only with the infrastructure and client flow necessary to fulfill your request. That includes the MCP client you connected, the hosting providers used to run the service, and Google APIs involved in the authorization and Search Console request lifecycle.

We do not share your Search Console data with third parties for independent marketing or resale purposes.

5. Retention and storage

OAuth credentials and operational data are retained only as long as needed to keep your connection working, meet security requirements, or comply with legal obligations. Search Console responses may pass through transient logs or short-lived processing systems, but the service is designed to avoid unnecessary long-term storage of Search Console content.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect account connections and service traffic. No internet service can guarantee absolute security, but we work to minimize access, limit scope, and protect tokens and request flows appropriately.

7. Your choices

  • You can stop using the service at any time.
  • You can revoke Google access from your Google account permissions screen.
  • You can request deletion of stored connection data, subject to legal and security obligations.

8. Children

This service is intended for professional and developer use and is not directed to children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will remain available at this URL with the latest effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or account-related requests, contact: privacy@irensaltali.com
